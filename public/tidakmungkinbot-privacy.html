Privacy Policy — Bang (Telegram Bot)

Last updated: 2026-02-04

Bang is an AI assistant bot that helps users respond to questions and execute chat-based requests.

Data we process

  • Messages you send to the bot (text and, if you send it, media).
  • Basic Telegram metadata required to reply (e.g. user_id / chat_id).
  • Technical/diagnostic data for reliability (e.g. timestamps, delivery status, error logs).

How we use data

  • To respond to you and perform the actions you request.
  • For troubleshooting, security, and service improvement.

Storage & retention

  • Messages and logs may be stored temporarily to maintain conversation context and for operational debugging.
  • We aim to retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes above.
  • We do not sell user data.

Third-party processing

  • Telegram processes messages according to its own policies.
  • Messages may be processed by third-party AI providers to generate responses.
  • We do not share your data with other parties except as required to operate the service.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data, but no system is 100% risk-free.

Your choices

  • Please avoid sending sensitive information (passwords, OTP codes, payment card data).
  • You can stop using the bot at any time.
  • You may request deletion of your data by contacting the bot owner.

Children

This bot is not intended for children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above will reflect changes.

Contact

Contact the bot owner (Bang Dany) on Telegram for any privacy-related questions.
